Wesleyan Hackathon Club

The Wesleyan University Hackathon Club (WesHacks) is a Computer Science interest group that connects Wes students interested in technology. WesHacks primarily exists as a club that provides students with an informational and social network. We foster and build a community amongst computer science students through club-hosted study breaks, meetings, and group hackathons at Wesleyan and other locations.

Our meetings run on Mondays, from 8 pm to 10 pm in 41 Wyllys, Room 113.

Sound Interesting?

Attend one of our meetings, and join our Facebook group ! We use these to announce club events, different hackathons we're attending, job opportunities, CS resources, and other computer-science related happenings.

Make sure to check out our events page to see what we're up to.

Finally, if you have any suggestions for cool things for the club to do (whether career development or just something fun), then please contact us!
